The 90‑Day GTM, Systems & AI Reset

When you're ready to rebuild the engine.

Many leaders start with advisory or a focused project and grow into a reset once we both see the opportunity.

Phase 1: Diagnose (Weeks 1–3)

  • Audit GTM (marketing, sales, product), systems, and reporting.
  • Map funnels, handoffs, and KPIs; find the 3–5 biggest leaks and constraints.
  • Prioritize AI, LLM, and Salesforce opportunities that matter in the next 6–12 months.

Phase 2: Design (Weeks 3–6)

  • Define your future‑state GTM model, processes, and scorecard.
  • Specify technology and AI changes in plain language.
  • Build a 6–12 month roadmap with owners, sequencing, and expected impact.

Phase 3: Implement & Hand Off (Weeks 6–12)

  • Lead or support implementation across GTM, AI, and systems.
  • Embed a new operating rhythm with your team.
  • Leave you with documentation, dashboards, and a clear "this is how we run growth now" model.
